📚 How Triple Predictor AI Works

📊 Step 1: Collect Data
Analyzes last 10 game results
📐 Step 2: Count Frequencies
Count LOW, MEDIUM, HIGH occurrences
🎯 Step 3: THREE Predictors
📐 MEDIAN: Middle value group
📈 HIGH-VOL: Most frequent group
📉 LOW-VOL: Least frequent group
✅ Step 4: Check Uniqueness
If median is UNIQUE → ALL THREE predict
⏳ Step 5: Handle Ties
If median duplicate → ALL THREE WAIT
🔄 Step 6: Shared Retry
Wrong prediction? ALL THREE recalculate with updated data
💡 Why Triple Predictor?
📐 MEDIAN: Resistant to outliers, represents central tendency
📈 HIGH-VOLUME: Follows the trend, predicts the most common outcome
📉 LOW-VOLUME: Contrarian approach, predicts the least common outcome
🔄 Shared WAITING & Retry: All three predictors wait together and retry together
